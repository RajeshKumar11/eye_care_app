# Community Guidelines

## Our Commitment

Eye Care App is an open-source project dedicated to helping people maintain healthy vision habits. We are committed to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all contributors, regardless of background or experience level.

## Standards

### Positive Behavior

We encourage:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping newcomers get started
- Being patient with questions
- Acknowledging the contributions of others

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:
- Harassment or discriminatory comments
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Personal attacks on contributors or users
- Spam or off-topic posts
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with these guidelines.

## Scope

These guidelines apply within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project or its community. Examples include:
- Using project communication channels (GitHub, discussions, etc.)
- Representing the project at events or online
- Acting as an appointed representative

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the project team at:
- **Email**: kakumanurajeshkumar@gmail.com

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is committed to maintaining confidentiality regarding the reporter of an incident.

### Consequences

Project maintainers will determine the consequences for any action they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. Consequences may include:

1. **Correction**: A private written warning explaining the nature of the violation
2. **Warning**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior
3. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary restriction from interaction with the community
4. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community interaction

## Values We Uphold

### Collaboration
We work together to build better software. Collaboration means:
- Asking questions when unsure
- Documenting solutions for others
- Sharing knowledge and helping others learn
- Building on each other's work respectfully

### Quality
We strive for:
- Well-tested, reliable code
- Clear documentation
- Thoughtful review and feedback
- Continuous improvement

### Respect
We treat everyone with respect:
- Assume positive intent
- Be patient with different skill levels
- Value diverse perspectives
- Acknowledge contributions of all sizes

### Transparency
We operate openly:
- Discussions happen in public channels
- Decisions are explained
- Processes are documented
- Everyone can participate
